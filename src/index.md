# ScriptSense

**ScriptSense** is a project-aware static analysis and workflow tool for Roblox Studio, designed to bring professional-grade tooling to Luau development.

## Why ScriptSense?

- **Type Safety**: Catch bugs before you run your game with advanced type checking.
- **Workflow Automation**: Automate repetitive tasks with custom scripts and hooks.
- **Project Awareness**: Understands your entire DataModel, not just individual scripts.
